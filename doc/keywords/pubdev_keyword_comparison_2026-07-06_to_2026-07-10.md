## Net Assessment

- The three new map providers achieved strong exact link/deeplink discoverability immediately, even without being named in the pubspec description.
- Baseline rankings are broadly stable: improvements and regressions are nearly balanced, and most existing positions are unchanged.
- The strongest newly discovered human intent is fallback, typed APIs, and store-provider navigation; these phrases frequently rank #1.
- Generic map-choice/coordinate language, WhatsApp task wording, social generic terms, and competitor-alternative phrases remain the largest content gaps.
- The next metadata/content pass should prioritize map selection and coordinates, WhatsApp Click to Chat/prefilled messages, restoring "without url_launcher" phrasing, and LinkedIn intent.
