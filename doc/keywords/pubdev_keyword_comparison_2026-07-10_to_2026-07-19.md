## Net Assessment

- Exact app-name/link/deeplink intent remains the most dependable app-specific surface, especially for regional navigation providers.
- The Threads action expansion tests whether newly published API wording is discoverable beyond the package name; its four rows should remain in future baselines.
- Likes and downloads improved while package metadata stayed fixed, offering a cleaner view of authority/indexing movement than the July 10 publish window.
- Generic map-coordinate tasks, WhatsApp task wording, and explicit competitor alternatives remain the most important content gaps when their related-group counts stay flat or negative.
- Future content should prefer concrete developer questions and API verbs over adding more near-duplicate generic keywords.
