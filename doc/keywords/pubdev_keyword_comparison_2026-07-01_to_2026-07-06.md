## Net Assessment

- The publish materially improved app-name and app-link visibility, especially WhatsApp, Telegram, Instagram, YouTube, and Amap.
- High-intent deeplink terms mostly held steady at #1.
- Some generic external/social terms moved down or disappeared, likely because the description now emphasizes named providers rather than generic social/external wording.
- Store fallback remains strong, but some exact fallback phrases moved from #1 to lower page-1 positions.
- The next metadata/content opportunity is WhatsApp message phrasing, `show navigation`, generic social wording, and `url_launcher` comparison phrasing.
